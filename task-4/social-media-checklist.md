✅ Do’s for Responsible Social Media Use
*Think before you post – Ensure your content is respectful and won’t harm your reputation.
*Maintain privacy settings – Regularly check and update your account privacy controls.
*Verify information before sharing – Avoid spreading misinformation or fake news.
*Use social media for learning and networking – Follow educational pages, professionals, and career opportunities.
*Be respectful in interactions – Engage politely, even when you disagree with others.

❌ Don’ts for Responsible Social Media Use
*Don’t overshare personal information – Avoid posting your address, phone number, or sensitive details.
*Don’t engage in cyberbullying or trolling – Negative behavior can harm others and your own image.
*Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content – This can affect your academic and career prospects.
*Don’t waste excessive time scrolling – Manage your time to stay productive.
*Don’t blindly trust everything online – Be critical of sources and content authenticity.
