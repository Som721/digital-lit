Stay Safe Online – Checklist
General Digital Safety
☐ Use strong, unique passwords for each account (mix letters, numbers, symbols); consider a password manager.
☐ Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking apps.
☐ Keep your devices and apps updated regularly to fix security vulnerabilities.
☐ Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities (banking, logins); use mobile data or a VPN instead.
☐ Don’t click on unknown links or attachments in emails, messages, or DMs—verify the sender first.
☐ Limit personal information shared on social media (address, phone number, college ID, location).

Financial / UPI Safety (Important)

☐ Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or CVV with anyone—even if they claim to be from the bank.
☐ Double-check UPI payment requests—fraudsters often send “collect requests” instead of payments.
☐ Use official apps only (like trusted banking/UPI apps) downloaded from verified app stores.
☐ Avoid scanning random QR codes; scammers may use them to request money instead of sending it.

Account & Privacy Protection
☐ Log out from shared/public computers and don’t save passwords on them.
☐ Review app permissions and deny unnecessary access (camera, contacts, location).
☐ Be cautious of fake job/internship offers asking for registration fees or personal details.
☐ Regularly back up important data (assignments, documents) to a secure cloud or drive.

Report Cybercrime in India
National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
Helpline: 1930

Report immediately in case of fraud, hacking, cyberbullying, or financial scams—quick action can help recover losses.
